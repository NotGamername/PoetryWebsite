The Poetry of M.P. Hamm

~ A brief introduction ~

My name is Maximilien Hein. You might be curious about the origins of my pen-name, M.P. Hamm. M.P. stands for Max Power, and my initials backwards are H.A.M., so Hamm is a variation on that. Thus we are left with M.P. Hamm!
